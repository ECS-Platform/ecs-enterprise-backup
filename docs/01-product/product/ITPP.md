# Framework Reference — ITPP

**Grounding:** `framework_catalog._itpp_catalog()`, route `/framework/ITPP`. **Status:** Catalog framework. Part of [Frameworks Library](README.md).

## Purpose
IT Policy & Procedures compliance — adherence to documented IT governance policies and standards.

## Objectives
Policy coverage, periodic review, exception governance, change/access policy adherence, documentation currency.

## Controls (catalog, sample)
Policy existence & approval · Periodic policy review · Change management policy adherence · Access policy adherence · Documentation currency.

## Checklist (sample)
- [ ] Policies approved and current
- [ ] Annual policy review complete
- [ ] Change/access policy adherence evidenced
- [ ] Exceptions tracked + approved

## Evidence Requirements
Policy documents, review minutes, adherence attestations, exception records.

## Control & Evidence Reuse
Policy/change/access evidence reuse with **ISO27001, RBI Cyber, CSITE**.

## Reporting
- **Executive:** policy compliance posture.
- **Audit:** policy adherence pack.
- **Risk:** policy gaps/exceptions to Risk Register.

## Sample Assessment / Findings / Closure
- **Assessment:** policies current; reviews complete.
- **Finding:** one policy overdue for review.
- **Closure:** reviewed + re-approved → Approved.
