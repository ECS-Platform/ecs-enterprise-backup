# ECS 3-Year Product Roadmap

Six releases over three years (≈ two releases per year). Each release lists **Capabilities**,
**Business Outcomes**, **Target Users**, and **Expected ROI**. ROI figures align with the ECS
ROI model (₹17.27 Cr live → ₹143.1 Cr net benefit at scale, ₹2.2 Cr stable OPEX, ~22.7 FTE).

Theme arc: **Harden → Automate → Intelligence → Platform → Scale → Leadership.**

---

## Release 1 — "Executive-Ready Core" (Q1–Q2 / Year 1 H1)
**Theme:** Harden the MVP into a production executive product.
- **Capabilities:** Board-mode presentation view, geo/Pan-India heatmap drill, SLA engine +
  escalation, audit calendar automation, finding→remediation workflow, framework crosswalk
  (v1), evidence/observation-linked reporting, coverage-gap analytics, scheduled collection,
  bulk approve/reject, executive scorecard analytics.
- **Business Outcomes:** Demo-to-production; faster audit prep; reduced manual reporting.
- **Target Users:** CIO, Head Audit, Compliance, App Owners.
- **Expected ROI:** ₹17–25 Cr annual value at current live footprint; ~40% audit-prep time cut.

## Release 2 — "Automation & Integration" (Q3–Q4 / Year 1 H2)
**Theme:** Autonomous collection and enterprise integration.
- **Capabilities:** Async collection workers, ServiceNow/Jira/GitHub connectors, REST API + SDK,
  SSO/SCIM, observability (OTel + dashboards), continuous control testing (v1), regulatory
  change feed, audit summarization (AI), evidence Q&A with citations, scheduled board-pack
  distribution, control ownership RACI, immutable governance audit trail.
- **Business Outcomes:** Evidence collection without manual chasing; enterprise-grade auth/ops.
- **Target Users:** Operations Owner, Platform Ops, CISO, Compliance.
- **Expected ROI:** +₹10–15 Cr incremental; ~60% reduction in evidence-chasing effort.

## Release 3 — "Intelligence" (Q1–Q2 / Year 2 H1)
**Theme:** AI and analytics that anticipate.
- **Capabilities:** Visual no-code workflow builder, predictive audit-readiness, predictive risk
  scoring + trajectory, agentic remediation drafting, AI evaluation/guardrail dashboard,
  anomaly detection, NL report builder, attestation campaigns, policy management lifecycle,
  connector marketplace (v1).
- **Business Outcomes:** Shift from reactive to predictive; AI-assisted remediation.
- **Target Users:** CRO, Governance Lead, Auditor, AI Governance Owner.
- **Expected ROI:** +₹20–30 Cr; measurable reduction in repeat observations and SLA breaches.

## Release 4 — "Enterprise Platform" (Q3–Q4 / Year 2 H2)
**Theme:** Platform breadth and ecosystem.
- **Capabilities:** SIEM + cloud-posture connectors, custom report designer, third-party/vendor
  risk, segregation-of-duties checks, mobile approvals, board risk committee workspace,
  multi-entity rollup dashboard, exec alerting/digest, workflow analytics.
- **Business Outcomes:** Single pane across security, cloud, vendors; mobile-enabled leadership.
- **Target Users:** CISO, CRO, Board Risk Committee, Vendor Management.
- **Expected ROI:** +₹30–45 Cr; consolidation of point GRC tools.

## Release 5 — "Scale & Multi-Tenancy" (Q1–Q2 / Year 3 H1)
**Theme:** Scale to the full estate and managed-service model.
- **Capabilities:** Multi-tenancy, regulatory submission generator, what-if scenario simulator,
  emerging-risk radar, AI control-test generation, data retention/lifecycle policy,
  point-in-time "time-machine" dashboards.
- **Business Outcomes:** Estate-wide scale; potential managed-service / multi-entity offering.
- **Target Users:** Enterprise CIO/CTO, group entities, managed-service operators.
- **Expected ROI:** Approaching ₹90–145 Cr net benefit at full scale on stable ₹2.2 Cr OPEX.

## Release 6 — "Market Leadership" (Q3–Q4 / Year 3 H2)
**Theme:** Differentiated, benchmarked, ecosystem leader.
- **Capabilities:** Benchmark-vs-peer analytics, AI-drafted board narratives at scale, deep
  ecosystem partner integrations, continuous-audit mode, advanced governance automation.
- **Business Outcomes:** Category differentiation; regulator-grade assurance; partner ecosystem.
- **Target Users:** Board, regulators, ecosystem partners.
- **Expected ROI:** Sustained ₹143.1 Cr+ net benefit; defensible competitive moat.

## Roadmap-at-a-glance

| Release | Theme | Headline ROI | Primary users |
|---|---|---|---|
| R1 | Executive-Ready Core | ₹17–25 Cr | CIO, Audit, Compliance |
| R2 | Automation & Integration | +₹10–15 Cr | Ops, CISO |
| R3 | Intelligence | +₹20–30 Cr | CRO, Governance, AI Gov |
| R4 | Enterprise Platform | +₹30–45 Cr | CISO, CRO, Board Risk |
| R5 | Scale & Multi-Tenancy | ₹90–145 Cr at scale | Group CIO/CTO |

## Dependencies & sequencing notes
- Async workers (R2) precede continuous control testing (R3) and predictive analytics (R3).
- REST API/SDK + connector marketplace (R2–R3) underpin ecosystem integrations (R4+).
- Multi-tenancy (R5) is the gate for any managed-service / group-entity expansion.
