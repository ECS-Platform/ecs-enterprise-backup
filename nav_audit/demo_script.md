# ECS — 10-Minute CIO Demonstration Script

Audience: CIO, CFO, Audit Committee, Board.
Goal: Show that ECS turns audit & compliance from a manual, recurring cost into an
automated, evidence-reuse engine with board-level visibility.
Format: Manual navigation, no auto-advance. Login as **CIO** persona (`?role=cio&user=cio`).

Timing target: 10 minutes. Each section lists the **screen**, the **say**, and the **do**.

---

## 0. Pre-demo setup (before the room)
- Browser at 1440px+ (laptop) or 1920px (boardroom display), 100% zoom.
- Land on **Executive Overview → Enterprise** as CIO.
- Copilot defaults to the collapsed bar (charts fully visible) — leave it collapsed until Section 8.

---

## 1. Opening Executive Narrative — 60s
**Screen:** Enterprise Governance Overview (CIO Summary strip visible).
**Say:** "Every year we run the same audits — VAPT, PCI DSS, IS Audit, baseline reviews —
across hundreds of applications. The effort is manual, repetitive, and invisible to
leadership until something slips. ECS changes that. Collect evidence once, reuse it across
every framework, and give this board a live view of audit readiness."
**Do:** Point at the CIO Summary strip: **84.6% Enterprise Compliance · 82.2% National Score
· 212 Open Observations · 91.6% Audit Complete**.

## 2. Enterprise Overview Walkthrough — 75s
**Screen:** Enterprise → Overview tab.
**Say:** "This is the enterprise posture in one screen — business units, compliance,
audit readiness, open gaps, observations and risk score, each with a trend line."
**Do:** Walk across the 3 Banking Business Unit cards (Retail 84.2%, Corporate 79.6%,
Digital Channels 72.4%) and the chart row. Highlight the trend pills (e.g. "+14% vs start").

## 3. Drilldown Journey — 75s
**Screen:** Enterprise → Open Gaps / any KPI card.
**Say:** "Nothing here is a static slide. Every number is a live drill into the underlying
evidence and observations."
**Do:** Click **Open Observations (212)** KPI → modal opens with contextual records
(application, framework, control, owner, status). Click a **chart bar** → same contextual
drill. Show the export options (CSV/XLSX/PDF). Close.

## 4. Framework Analysis — 60s
**Screen:** Enterprise → Frameworks tab.
**Say:** "Coverage by framework — PCI DSS, VAPT, OS/DB baselining, AppSec and more. This is
where a framework owner sees their gaps."
**Do:** Show the 15 frameworks with maturity %. Click a framework row → drill to the
controls/evidence behind it.

## 5. Pan India View — 75s
**Screen:** Executive Overview → Pan India → Analytics (regional heatmap).
**Say:** "Compliance isn't uniform. This heatmap shows it by zone — green is healthy,
amber and red need attention."
**Do:** Point at the heatmap: North 87.4% (green), South 91.3% (green), East 83.2% (amber),
Central 78.8% (amber), West 71.3% (red). Click **West** → drill into the region's
applications and breaches. Mention **5 regions · 6,225 branches · 36 SLA breaches**.

## 6. Reports Module — 60s
**Screen:** Executive Overview → Reports → Observation Detail / Scheduled.
**Say:** "Audit-ready exports, on a schedule, linked to the actual observations and evidence —
not hand-built decks."
**Do:** Show the Observation Detail table (realistic banking observations, 35 rows, paginated)
and the Scheduled tab (PCI DSS Executive Audit Pack — Weekly, VAPT Closure — Quarterly).

## 7. Trends Module — 60s
**Screen:** Executive Overview → Trends → Coverage / SLA / Evidence Aging.
**Say:** "The direction of travel matters more than any single month. Coverage is climbing,
rejections are falling, SLA on-time is up to 91%."
**Do:** Show Coverage (Nov 73% → Mar 81%), SLA trend (on-time 82% → 91%, breaches 18 → 8),
and Evidence Aging (avg ~23 days, benchmark line). Note the forecasting/velocity charts.

## 8. AI Copilot Demonstration — 75s
**Screen:** Any module; expand the collapsed **✦ ECS AI Copilot** bar.
**Say:** "Leadership shouldn't have to know where to click. Ask in plain language."
**Do:** Expand Copilot. Use a quick-ask chip (e.g. "Why is Net Banking down?" / "Show DR
readiness" / "PCI DSS"). Show the response with linked references/evidence. Collapse it again
to restore the full dashboard.

## 9. Business Value Discussion — 75s
**Screen:** Executive Overview → ROI & Value Realization.
**Say:** "Here's the economics. Live value today is **₹17.27 Cr**. At scale, ECS returns a
**₹143.1 Cr net benefit** on a stable **₹2.2 Cr** annual operating cost — productivity
equivalent to roughly **22.7 full-time employees** returned to the business each year."
**Do:** Walk the 4 ROI slides (Live FY25-26 value → Framework master → FTE productivity →
Executive value dashboard). Show the scenario badges (Conservative / Expected / Aggressive).

## 10. Closing Summary — 45s
**Screen:** Back to Enterprise Overview.
**Say:** "One platform. Collect once, reuse everywhere. Live audit readiness for the board,
automation for the teams, and a clear return on investment. ECS is ready to scale across the
application estate."
**Do:** End on the CIO Summary strip. Invite questions.
