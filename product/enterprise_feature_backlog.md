**Suggested Release (R1–R6)**.

Legend — BV/C: H=High, M=Medium, L=Low. Priority: Must / Should / Nice.
Releases map to the 3-year roadmap (R1≈Q1, R6≈Q12).

## Prioritization summary
- **Must-have (R1–R2 core):** SLA engine, framework crosswalk, scheduled collection, audit
  calendar, evidence/observation-linked reporting, board-mode, geo heatmap, REST API/SDK, SSO,
  observability, async workers, audit summarization, evidence Q&A.
- **Should-have (R3–R4 differentiation):** workflow designer, continuous control testing,
  predictive risk, connector marketplace, policy management, agentic remediation.
- **Nice-to-have (R5–R6 leadership):** what-if simulation, multi-tenancy, regulatory submission
  generation, emerging-risk radar.
